What these mean
Calls/day × Days in field = visits one rep can do per year. Cycles/yr only multiplies the target visits if your "Call Vist No" is per cycle — leave at 1 if it is already a yearly number.

Utilisation = share of a rep's theoretical capacity actually delivered as target calls. If reps reliably hit ~100% of plan, set this near 100%. Lower values build in a buffer for admin, travel and leave.

Cost behaviour
50%
What this means
How much of your non-rep costs (cost of goods, marketing, distribution, office, admin) go up and down with sales.

100% = all costs move with sales: if sales drop 20%, these costs drop ~20%, so profit % barely changes and you hardly need to cut reps (easy case).

0% = none move (all fixed): costs stay the same when sales fall, so the whole profit drop lands on the sales team and you'd cut far more reps (hard case).

Not sure? Pick a preset and check the band on the right — it shows both extremes, so you see how much this assumption matters before confirming with Finance.
